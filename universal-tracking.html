Universal Tracking works by segmenting audience into different customer cohorts

New Shopper

Customers who have installed your app and completed registration but have not made a transaction as of yet.

Drop cart customers

Customers who added products in the shopping cart but did not complete the transaction. Convert these users using dynamic ads using the browsed & similar products.

Lapsed or dormant shoppers

Activate dormant or lapsed users with targeted ads. Customers who have not made a purchase in the last 30 days or a specific period of time.

Hight LTV shoppers

Boost engagement with regular shoppers by targeting higher average order value or increasing the cart size. Upselling and Cross-selling product ads work wonders for this customer cohort.

Loyalty shoppers

Engage with loyal customers by using reminder ads in multiple creative formats. Remind them that you value them by running customised campaigns.

Lookalike modelling

Our machine learning algorithms work on the customers who have browsed the products recently and help you acquire new customers by understanding the audience segments that need retargetting based on the existing customer patterns.

The Recipie

  • The key is to understand the user's taste and preferences and then target them on appropriate products.
  • Here's an example - Oliver visits your website and checks out multiple products over time. Later that day, he opens entertainment blogs and starts absorbing the content before bed time.
  • Parallelly, our tag continues to build a user-profile, keeping a note of the websites he has visited, average time spent, content he has viewed and the ads he clicked.
  • Next day, Oliver casually browses the internet such as Facebook, Instagram as usual meanwhile our tag has started buying media placements through 3rd party ad selling agencies. Ultimately, Oliver gets displayed highly relevant ads on his smartphone or desktop including but not limited to exclusive coupon codes, discounts to prep up the buyer and convert him into a customer.
  • Since this is not an easy process, we take a huge amount of load/risk at our end wherein our automated checks keep on optimising the ads to be displayed to the user, all at the cost of standard 'Cost Per Sale/Action' Model in the Performance Marketing Industry.
  • Hence, we are rewarded when our clients are rewarded - customer acquisition!
